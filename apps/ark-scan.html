About

ArkScan is being developed as a more serious document app — not just a quick camera scanner, but a cleaner and more capable tool for people who regularly work with paper documents.

The goal is to combine scanning, editing, exporting, and document management into one experience that feels polished, organized, and practical for real everyday use.
