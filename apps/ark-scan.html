About

ArkScan is being developed as a polished document scanner for people who want more than a basic camera capture. The focus is on cleaner output, smoother editing, and better everyday document handling.

From receipts and notes to multi-page paperwork, ArkScan is intended to make scanning, exporting, and organizing documents feel faster, tidier, and more professional.
