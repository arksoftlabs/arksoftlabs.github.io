About

MIC Guard is made for people who want clearer awareness around microphone-related privacy on Android. It focuses on practical protection, simple guidance, and a straightforward user experience.

Instead of burying privacy behind technical complexity, MIC Guard aims to make microphone control and awareness feel understandable, accessible, and useful in daily use.
