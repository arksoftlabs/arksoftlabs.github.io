About

SoftBudget is designed for people who want a simple and reliable way to manage money without the noise of complex finance apps. It focuses on clarity, consistency, and everyday budgeting.

Whether you are tracking personal expenses or building stronger spending habits, SoftBudget aims to keep budgeting approachable, organized, and easy to maintain.
