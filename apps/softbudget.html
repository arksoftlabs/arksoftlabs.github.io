About

SoftBudget is built for people who want a simple, clear, and reliable way to manage their finances. Instead of complicated financial dashboards, SoftBudget focuses on clarity — helping you track spending, stay within your budget, and understand where your money goes.

Whether you're managing household expenses, personal spending, or just trying to build better financial habits, SoftBudget keeps everything simple and easy to maintain.
