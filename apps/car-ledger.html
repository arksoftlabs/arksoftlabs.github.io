About

Car Ledger is being shaped as a practical companion for vehicle owners who want a clear picture of what their car really costs over time.

Instead of scattered receipts and half-remembered service dates, the app aims to keep fuel, maintenance, and ownership history organized in one calm workflow.

Keywords: vehicle expense tracker, fuel log, maintenance tracker, service history, car cost tracker.
