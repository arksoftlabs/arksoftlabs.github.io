Highlights

  • Prayer reminders with adjustable notification sounds
  • Track missed prayers with a simple Qaza record system
  • Clean, focused, and privacy-friendly experience

About

Salah Companion is designed to make daily prayer easier to follow and easier to maintain. From checking the next prayer to finding the Qibla or reviewing missed prayers, everything is organized in a simple flow.

The goal is to offer a dependable Islamic utility app that feels clear, respectful, and practical for everyday use without unnecessary complexity.

Keywords: prayer times, Qibla direction, Qaza tracker, prayer reminder, Islamic app, Muslim prayer app.
