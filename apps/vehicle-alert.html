About

Vehicle Alerts is built for users who need timely awareness of important vehicle events without unnecessary complexity. It focuses on fast notifications, clarity, and a dependable monitoring experience.

Whether you are following a personal vehicle or monitoring operational activity, Vehicle Alerts helps you stay informed through immediate updates related to movement, ignition, speed, geofence activity, and power-related changes.
