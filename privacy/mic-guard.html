MIC Guard – Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-02-08

This Privacy Policy describes how MIC Guard (the “App”) by ArkSoft Labs handles user data. We aim to keep the App lightweight and privacy-friendly.

Data we collect

MIC Guard is designed to function without requiring personal information. The App may display ads (if enabled). Advertising partners may collect limited data to serve ads, such as device identifiers and ad interaction data, according to their policies.

Ads (AdMob)

If the App uses Google AdMob, Google may collect data for ad delivery, measurement, and fraud prevention. You can review Google’s policies in their documentation and your device ad settings.

App permissions

Easy Tasbeeh does not require sensitive permissions to operate in normal use. If permissions are added in future updates, this policy will be updated accordingly.

Children’s privacy

The App is not designed to knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided personal data, contact us to request deletion.

Changes

We may update this policy as the App evolves. The latest version will be posted on this page.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact ArkSoft Labs via the contact page: https://arksoftlabs.com/contact.html
